More about Concept Brand

Concept Clothing was founded in 2021 in Tzaneen by Cassius Rapau. What started as a small vision has grown into a fresh and inspiring clothing brand that blends creativity, comfort, and style. Cassius, the founder, has always been passionate about fashion and self-expression. His goal is to create clothing that speaks to individuality while remaining affordable and trendy. Through Concept Clothing, he hopes to inspire confidence and empower people to express who they are. At Concept Clothing, every design reflects originality and dedication — making fashion simple, bold, and unique.
